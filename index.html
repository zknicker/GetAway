Welcome to the island.

You've awoken on an island following a plane crash, and find yourself stranded with little resources in a dinosaur inhabited paradise. Weather the harsh conditions, collect and craft the survival tools you need, and ultimately attempt escape from the island. As quick as you can muster - get away!

Embark On An Adventure

Get ready for a vacation to a tropical island filled with creatures! Surely nothing will go wrong.

Explore Beautiful Landscape

Walk the huge procedurally generated island to explore new landscape. You get a fresh island ever playthrough.

Survive The Night

Campfires aid your survival with life-saving health rejuvenation and visibility, among other things.

The Threat Of Dinosaurs

Watch out for the dinosaurs inhabiting the island. Tyrannosaurus Rex are known to be particularly dangerous.

Craft Items

Craft new items from existing resources to get bigger and better tools. Crafting is essential to your survival.

Collect Resources

Collect resources, like the wood pictured here, to craft the necessary tools to survive and escape the island.

Scavenge For Food

Collect food from resources scatted across the island. Berries are a particularly interesting treat.

Roaming Dinosaur Packs

Angry packs of dinosaurs, fueled by the incredible "PackAI" technology, roam the island looking for you.

We're making progress every 2 weeks per the "scrum" development framework popular amongst modern day software development teams.

The most recent timebox brings a slew of improvements including:

  • A more fleshed out crafting system with additional items.
  • Collectible items around the island.
  • Roaming dinosaurs including the T-Rex and Raptor.
  • Choppable trees, placeable campfires, and a placeable raft.
  • Optimized island generation and overall optimizations to the game.

Meet The Team

Get Away was crafted by a team of students from The Ohio State University.

Quentin Bloomfield

One with the dinosaurs, Quentin spends his time crafting a convincing Dinosaur AI, and helping out with all other aspects of the game when he can.

Keegan Donnelly

Scrum master, tester, and the mastermind behind everything player related. Your character's very existence is owed to this dedicated crafter that is Keegan.

Jacob Grealey

Procedural environments are complex beasts, and Jacob is their master. Environment creation, engine optimization, and other computational tasks fall on his shoulders.

Zach Knickerbocker

Zach spends most of his time working on the game's items, crafting, and graphical UI elements - everything from the crafting menu to the player inventory.

Nate Niederkorn

The other half of the genius team behind our incredible Dinosaur AI, Nate dedicates himself to improving dinosaurs and helping out with other aspects of programming when he can.

Sean Peck

Sean has a knack for killer sound design. Sean also contributes to the player's interaction with the environment, playing a hugely important role in gamifying what is otherwise a dinosaur simulation.
